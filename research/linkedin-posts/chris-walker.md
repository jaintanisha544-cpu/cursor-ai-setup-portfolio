# Chris Walker

## Background

Chris Walker is the founder and CEO of Refine Labs, a B2B demand generation consultancy. He is known for challenging traditional lead generation tactics and promoting modern demand creation strategies that prioritize buyer education and long-term growth.

## Why Selected

Chris Walker was selected because he is one of the most influential voices in modern B2B SaaS marketing. His work focuses on aligning marketing efforts with actual buyer behavior and building sustainable demand generation systems.

## Key Content Themes

- Demand generation
- Buyer behavior
- Revenue marketing
- B2B SaaS growth
- Marketing measurement
- Customer acquisition
- Content-driven demand

## Content Pattern Analysis

Chris frequently challenges outdated marketing practices and encourages marketers to focus on business outcomes rather than vanity metrics.

His content often includes:

- Strong opinions supported by data
- Practical marketing frameworks
- Analysis of buyer journeys
- Revenue-focused strategies
- Real-world business examples
