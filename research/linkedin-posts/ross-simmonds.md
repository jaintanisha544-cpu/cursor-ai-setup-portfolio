# Ross Simmonds

## Background

Ross Simmonds is the founder and CEO of Foundation Marketing, a content marketing agency focused on content distribution and amplification. He is widely recognized for helping brands maximize the reach and impact of their content through strategic distribution systems.

## Why Selected

Ross Simmonds was selected because his expertise complements traditional content creation by focusing on content promotion and distribution. His work demonstrates how B2B SaaS companies can achieve greater ROI by ensuring content reaches the right audiences.

## Content Pattern Analysis

Ross frequently emphasizes that distribution is often more important than creation. His content typically includes:

- Detailed frameworks
- Distribution checklists

His posts are practical, execution-focused, and designed to help marketers achieve measurable business outcomes.
