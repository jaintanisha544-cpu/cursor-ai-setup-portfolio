# LinkedIn Content Frameworks for B2B SaaS

## Purpose

This document summarizes common content patterns observed across the selected B2B SaaS marketing experts.

The goal is to identify repeatable frameworks that can be used to build audience trust, increase visibility, and generate demand through LinkedIn organic content.

---

# Framework 1: Story + Lesson

Experts:

- Justin Welsh
- Tas Bober
- Devin Reed

Pattern:

1. Start with a personal story.
2. Introduce a challenge.
3. Share a lesson learned.
4. End with a practical takeaway.

Why It Works:

Stories capture attention while lessons create value.

Application:

Founders and marketers can share real business experiences to build credibility and audience trust.

---

# Framework 2: Problem → Solution

Experts:

- Chris Walker
- Dave Gerhardt
- Gaetano DiNardi

Pattern:

1. Identify a common industry problem.
2. Explain why traditional approaches fail.
3. Introduce a better solution.
4. Provide actionable advice.

Why It Works:

Readers immediately recognize the problem and become interested in the solution.

Application:

Ideal for demand generation and educational marketing content.

---

# Framework 3: Data + Insight

Experts:

- Amanda Natividad
- Kyle Poyar

Pattern:

1. Present data or research.
2. Explain its meaning.
3. Connect it to business outcomes.
4. Recommend actions.

Why It Works:

Data increases credibility and encourages sharing.

Application:

Useful for thought leadership and industry trend content.

---

# Framework 4: Contrarian Opinion

Experts:

- Chris Walker
- Amanda Natividad
- Ross Simmonds

Pattern:

1. Challenge a common belief.
2. Present evidence.
3. Explain a new perspective.
4. Support the argument with examples.

Why It Works:

Creates curiosity and drives engagement.

Application:

Useful for building authority and differentiated positioning.

---

# Framework 5: Actionable Framework

Experts:

- Justin Welsh
- Devin Reed
- Ross Simmonds

Pattern:

1. Introduce a goal.
2. Present a step-by-step framework.
3. Explain implementation.
4. Share expected outcomes.

Why It Works:

Readers can immediately apply the advice.

Application:

Highly effective for engagement and saves audiences time.

---

# Key Findings

Across all 10 experts, the most common success factors were:

- Consistency
- Educational content
- Audience-first thinking
- Personal credibility
- Clear frameworks
- Long-term trust building

The strongest content focused on helping the audience solve problems rather than promoting products directly.

---

# Recommended LinkedIn Strategy for B2B SaaS

1. Publish educational content consistently.
2. Focus on audience needs before product promotion.
3. Use storytelling to improve engagement.
4. Create repeatable content systems.
5. Repurpose successful content.
6. Measure business impact rather than vanity metrics.
7. Build trust before attempting conversion.
